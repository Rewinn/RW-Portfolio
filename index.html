Nice To Meet You

Hello!



Regardless of the device or the size of the screen, I can produce the content.

As a producer, my job is to deliver content that attracts a customer, engages the audience and creates an emotional reaction. My work is high quality, on time and always within budget. I love what I do.

Someone once asked me what a producer does. My response: “Anything and everything.” I liken my role as producer to that of an explorer leading an expedition through a jungle. We have no idea what we’re up against, something will come out of nowhere to throw us off course, but we must keep moving forward.

I’ve been told people have fun spending eight+ hours a day with me, my favorite movie of all time is Paper Moon, and I’m an excellent pinball player.
